Incident Report — Synthetic Case File SF-2021-027
Facility: Steel Processing Plant, Zone 2
Date: November 2021 (illustrative synthetic case, for demo purposes)

Summary: A pressure buildup in a coolant line coincided with a shift
changeover. The outgoing shift noted a minor pressure anomaly in the
handover log, but the incoming shift did not review it before beginning
routine operations. Pressure continued climbing for another 25 minutes
before an automatic safety valve released it, causing a loud but
non-injurious venting event that briefly halted production.

Root cause: Handover logs were not cross-referenced against live sensor
trends. A minor anomaly noted informally in a log was never escalated to
an active alert.

Lesson: Shift changeovers combined with any unresolved sensor anomaly in
the same zone should be treated as elevated risk, not routine handover.
