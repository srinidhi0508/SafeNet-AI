Incident Report — Synthetic Case File SF-2022-011
Facility: Chemical Processing Unit, Zone 3
Date: July 2022 (illustrative synthetic case, for demo purposes)

Summary: A worker was found without required respiratory protective
equipment in a zone with moderate but sub-threshold gas readings. CCTV
footage later showed the worker had entered nearly ten minutes before
being noticed by a supervisor doing a routine walk-through.

Root cause: PPE compliance was only checked via periodic manual
walk-throughs, not continuously monitored.

Lesson: Automated PPE detection at zone entry points, combined with
current gas readings for that zone, would have flagged the entry
immediately rather than after a ten-minute delay.
